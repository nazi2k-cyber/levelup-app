🎮 Turn your real life into an RPG adventure — Level Up for real!

LEVEL UP: REBOOT is a self-improvement app that gamifies your daily life using RPG mechanics. Complete quests, grow your stats, and raid dungeons to become the best version of yourself.

─────────────────────────────
⚔️ KEY FEATURES
─────────────────────────────

📊 Hunter Stat System
• 6 core stats: STR · INT · CHA · VIT · WLT · AGI
• Stats grow in real-time as you complete quests
• Earn titles as you level up — from "New Awakened" to "Legendary Hunter"

📋 Weekly Quests
• 12 daily quests across categories: exercise, study, diet, finance, and more
• New quests every week to keep things fresh
• Complete quests → earn points & stat bonuses instantly

🏰 Global Dungeon Raids
• Team up with hunters worldwide on weekly dungeon raids
• Your contributions count toward global raid progress in real-time
• Clear dungeons together to claim special rewards

👥 Social Ranking & Friends
• Global and friends leaderboards for competitive motivation
• Add friends and compare your stats
• Weekly top hunter spotlight

📍 Step Tracking (GPS / Health Connect)
• Syncs with Google Fit & Health Connect for real step counting
• Earn AGI (Agility) stat automatically when you hit your step goals

🗓️ Planner & Day1 Feed
• Plan your day with priority tasks & timetable
• Share your daily story with photos on the Day1 feed
• Save planner as image & share via link

🛠️ Smart Tools
• 1RM Calculator — Track your big 3 lifts (squat, bench, deadlift)
• Meditation Timer — Focus with singing bowl sounds
• D-Day Counter — Track important goal dates
• My Library — Manage your reading list (Babel's Library)
• Running Calculator — Auto-calculate pace, distance & time
• Life Progress — Visualize your remaining time
• Pomodoro Timer — Focused work sessions with break intervals
• Big5 Personality Test — Discover your personality type & earn CHA stat
• My Movies — Track films you've watched & want to see
• Challenges & Roulette — Daily bonus missions with random rewards

🌍 Multilingual Support
• Korean · English · Japanese
• Switch languages anytime within the app
